Detailed Description

Architecture definition of the detection_logic.

Transitions of the input signal data_i are being listen to. Because the data_i signal can change within a flip-flop's setup and hold times the output may be unusable for various reasons. The flip-flop may or may not have propagated the new value, or perhaps got confused for a while and not settled to any real output value ("metastability"). So we do synchronization with two-stage shift-register that are clocked by the target domain's clock. In this way we get a synchronous version of that asynchronous input signal. Each time transition of data_i is detected, depending if it was rising or faling edge, the pair of rising/falling outputs are being updated. ?_ts_h refers to the Unix time moment and ?_ts_l to the nanosec moment of transition.
