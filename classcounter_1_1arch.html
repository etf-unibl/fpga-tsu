Detailed Description

Architecture definition of the counter.

The frequency of the clock signal is defined as 50MHz Based on that, the step increment for time is 20ns, or the period of the clock signal. The nanosecond counter increments itself by 20 every clock cycle. When it reaches 1000000000, one second has passed in real time, and the Unix counter gets incremented by one, and the nanoseconds counter gets reset
