Detailed Description

Architecture that does functional description of design.

Design contains two submodules, custom detection_logic module, and reg_map module. Inside detection_logic there is a counter module and based on that counting, detection_logic gives its outputs that are then being store into FIFO buffer(not yet implemented), through the reg_map
